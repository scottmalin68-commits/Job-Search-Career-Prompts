**Author:** Scott M.

## Purpose
The Universal Interview Architect isn't a "resume matcher." It is a **Reverse Engineering Engine**. It identifies the specific anxieties of a hiring team, translates corporate jargon into "Unwritten Rules," and builds an evidence-based defense strategy tailored to who you are talking to (Agency, Internal, or Hiring Manager).

## What the Engine Gathers
To get a High-Fidelity output, the Architect synthesizes five core data streams:
1.  **The Persona:** Identifies if the goal is *Marketability* (Agency), *Compliance* (Internal), or *Problem-Solving* (Manager).
2.  **The Fear Factor:** Locates the specific bottleneck or risk the company is trying to fix by hiring you.
3.  **The Unwritten Rules:** Translates abstract values (like "Bias for Action") into reality (e.g., "Expect chaos and no documentation").
4.  **The Reality Gap:** Identifies the friction between the "Cutting Edge" job post and the "Legacy" technical debt you’ll actually be managing.
5.  **Evidence-Based Proof:** Strips buzzwords and replaces them with hard metrics from your Career Profile.

## The "Micro-Query" Toolkit
Once the `InterviewPrep-` file is generated, do not read the whole document during the call. Use these **Lightweight Extraction Queries** to pull exactly what you need in seconds. 

### 1. The Opening Hook
> **Query:** "Based on the prep file, give me my 90-second connection for [Agency/Internal/Hiring Manager]."
* **Why:** Pulls the narrative arc specifically tuned for the person on the screen.

### 2. The "Bragging" Statements
> **Query:** "What are my 3 top 'Fluff-to-Fact' metrics for this role?"
* **Why:** Perfect for when an agency recruiter asks, "Why should I present you to my client?"

### 3. The BS-Detector (Values Test)
> **Query:** "What are my 2 'Value-Validation' questions for this manager?"
* **Why:** Pulls the questions designed to see if the company actually lives its stated culture.

### 4. Technical Pivot (The Bridge)
> **Query:** "How do I explain my legacy experience against their new tech stack?"
* **Why:** Pulls the strategy from 'The Reality Gap' section to prove you can handle the transition.

### 5. Defensive Maneuvers
> **Query:** "Check my CV Landmines. What is the direct PlainTalk defense for [Gap/Tenure/Skill]?"
* **Why:** Gives you the punchy, non-defensive answer for your weakest points.

## Hallucination Guardrail
This tool is built on **Data Sufficiency Logic**. 
* If the input data is missing or weak, the output will be tagged as **[Insufficient Data]**. 
* **Rule:** If you see this tag, do not invent an answer. Use the "Micro-Query" to ask: *"What evidence am I missing to prove [Skill]?"* and then provide that info to the AI to regenerate the section.

---

## Best Practices
1.  **Feed the Beast:** The better your "Recon Intelligence" and "Career Profile" inputs, the more lethal the output.
2.  **Stay PlainTalk:** The prompt is hard-coded to avoid "AI-isms." If the output sounds like a robot, verify your "Style Constraints" are active.
3.  **The 90-Second Rule:** Never let the "Tell me about yourself" answer go longer than 90 seconds. The Architect is tuned for brevity.
