## What the Engine Gathers
To get a High-Fidelity output, the Architect synthesizes six core data streams:
1.  **The Persona:** Identifies if the goal is *Marketability* (Agency), *Compliance* (Internal), *Problem-Solving* (Manager), or **Algorithm Alignment (AI Bot)**.
2.  **The Fear Factor:** Locates the specific bottleneck or risk the company is trying to fix.
3.  **The Unwritten Rules:** Translates abstract values into reality (e.g., "Agile" -> "Chaotic").
4.  **The Reality Gap:** Identifies friction between "Posted" tech and "Actual" technical debt.
5.  **Evidence-Based Proof:** Strips buzzwords and replaces them with hard metrics.
6.  **The Internal Defense:** Provides the "Why Scott?" rationale to de-risk the hiring decision.

## The "Bot-Runner" Logic
When facing an AI interview, the engine shifts focus from "vibe" to "signal." 
* **Keyword Density:** It prioritizes the exact terminology found in the Job Posting.
* **Structural Markers:** It ensures you explicitly say "The situation was..." and "The result was..." so the AI's NLP (Natural Language Processing) can categorize your answer correctly.
* **Brevity:** High word count is often penalized as "low clarity" by AI. Aim for 90–120 seconds max.

## Hallucination Guardrail
This tool uses **Data Sufficiency Logic**. 
* If the input data is missing, the output is tagged as **[Insufficient Data]**. 
* **Rule:** If you see this tag, do not wing it. Provide the missing context to regenerate.
