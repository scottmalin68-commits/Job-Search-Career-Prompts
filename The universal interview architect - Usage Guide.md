## Purpose
The Universal Interview Architect is a **Reverse Engineering Engine**. It identifies the specific anxieties of a hiring team, translates corporate jargon into "Unwritten Rules," and builds an evidence-based defense strategy tailored to the specific power dynamic of your interviewer.

## What the Engine Gathers
To get a High-Fidelity output, the Architect synthesizes five core data streams:
1.  **The Persona:** Identifies if the goal is *Marketability* (Agency), *Compliance* (Internal), or *Problem-Solving* (Manager).
2.  **The Fear Factor:** Locates the specific bottleneck or risk the company is trying to fix.
3.  **The Unwritten Rules:** Translates abstract values into reality (e.g., "Agile" -> "Chaotic").
4.  **The Reality Gap:** Identifies friction between "Posted" tech and "Actual" technical debt.
5.  **Evidence-Based Proof:** Strips buzzwords and replaces them with hard metrics.

## The Persona-Specific Query Toolkit
Use these "Micro-Queries" to pull the exact data you need in real-time based on who you are talking to.

### Type 1: The Agency Recruiter (The Talent Scout)
*Goal: Prove you are an "A-Player" they can easily sell for a commission.*
> **Query:** "Give me my 3 'Bragging Statements' and my 'Exit Message' for this Agency call."
> **Query:** "What is one piece of 'Market Intelligence' I can share to be a resource for this recruiter?"

### Type 2: The Internal Recruiter (The Gatekeeper)
*Goal: Check the boxes, mitigate risk, and fit the budget.*
> **Query:** "Give me the 'Box-Ticking' hook that maps my skills to the job's non-negotiables."
> **Query:** "What are the 2 'CV Landmines' I need to defend to stay in the running?"

### Type 3: The Hiring Manager (The Decision Maker)
*Goal: Demonstrate peer-level problem solving and technical leadership.*
> **Query:** "Give me the 'Problem-Solving' hook that addresses the Hiring Manager's specific Fear Factor."
> **Query:** "What are my 3 'Power Shift' questions to test their Unwritten Rules and Tech Debt?"

---

## The "Reality Gap" Extraction
If the interview gets deep into technical transitions or legacy systems:
> **Query:** "How do I frame my legacy experience as the 'bridge' to their future tech stack?"

---

## Hallucination Guardrail
This tool uses **Data Sufficiency Logic**. 
* If the input data is missing, the output is tagged as **[Insufficient Data]**. 
* **Rule:** If you see this tag, do not wing it. Use the query: *"What evidence is missing to prove [Skill]?"* then provide that info to the AI to regenerate.

---

## Best Practices
1.  **Persona First:** Always tell the AI who you are meeting with before extracting data.
2.  **The 90-Second Rule:** Keep the "Tell me about yourself" answer under 90 seconds.
3.  **Value Stress-Test:** Use the 'Power Shift' questions to see if the company actually lives its values before you sign.
