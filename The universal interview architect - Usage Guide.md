# USER GUIDE: The Universal Interview Architect
**Author:** Scott M.
**Version:** 3.5.0
**Date:** 2026-05-20

## Purpose
The Universal Interview Architect is a **Reverse Engineering Engine**. It destroys corporate fluff, identifies the specific anxieties of a hiring team, and builds an evidence-based strategy focused entirely on measurable ROI. It maps your experience directly to the specific power dynamic and perspective of your interviewer.

---

## CHANGELOG (v3.5.0)
* **The Stakeholder Persona Filter:** Pivots responses based on the specific interviewer type: Recruiter (Gatekeeper), Hiring Manager (Day-to-day value), or Skip-Level (Strategy/Vision).
* **The KPI Receipt Library:** Replaced generic metrics with a hard requirement to map claims to 15 specific industry KPIs. Missing links are tagged **[Weak Evidence]**.
* **Hardened Vocabulary Firewall:** Strictly bans 30 overused "AI-style" buzzwords and replaces them with clean, direct, high-impact alternatives.
* **The Gap Probe:** Automated the analysis of job posting "gray areas," hidden technical risks, and resume deficiencies.
* **Aggressive Brevity Filter:** Hardened the response constraint down to a target of ≤60 words, with an absolute maximum of 80 words per answer.

---

## What the Engine Gathers
To get a High-Fidelity output, the Architect synthesizes five core data streams:
1.  **The Industry Pain Guess:** Locates the specific "fire" or critical bottleneck the company is desperate to put out.
2.  **The Reality Gap:** Pinpoints where the written JD likely differs from the actual daily, chaotic environment.
3.  **The Stakeholder Lens:** Decodes exactly what the interviewer is terrified of based on their specific corporate role.
4.  **Evidence-Based Proof:** Strips away weak resume filler and enforces the **3-Step Validation** process (Vignette, Approach, Payoff).
5.  **CV Landmines:** Identifies high-risk gaps in your profile and provides blunt, bulletproof defenses.

## The Stakeholder-Specific Query Toolkit

### Type 1: The Recruiter (The Gatekeeper)
*Goal: Clear the compliance hurdle, show high marketability, and defuse immediate profile risks.*
> **Query:** "Give me my 'Opening Line' and the 2 'CV Landmines' defenses for this Recruiter call."
> **Query:** "Run 'Why did you leave?' for the Recruiter lens. Keep it under 60 words."

### Type 2: The Hiring Manager (The Decision Maker)
*Goal: Prove day-to-day technical value, address immediate technical debt, and deliver a high-impact metric.*
> **Query:** "What is the 'One Thing to Hammer' for the Hiring Manager? Give me the specific KPI receipt."
> **Query:** "Give me the 'Tell me about yourself' response tailored for the Hiring Manager's day-to-day pain."

### Type 3: The Skip-Level / Executive (The Strategist)
*Goal: Align with long-term business strategy, process efficiency, high-level vision, and cross-functional leadership.*
> **Query:** "Give me my 3 'Power Shift' questions to test their long-term strategy and industry trends."
> **Query:** "Run 'Why should we hire you?' tailored for the Skip-Level lens, focusing on strategic ROI."

## The "Vocabulary Firewall" Protocol
The engine actively monitors and filters out weak, overused text. If a banned phrase slips in, it is immediately executed and replaced:
·  **Hardworking** → Driven / Committed
·  **Team player** → Collaborative
·  **Problem solver** → Analytical / Solution-oriented
·  **Helped** → Facilitated / Enabled
·  **Worked** → Executed / Delivered
·  **Quick learner** → Agile / Fast-learning
·  **Self-starter** → Proactive / Initiative-taking
·  **Used** → Leveraged / Applied

---

## Data Confidence Guardrail
This tool uses an aggressive **Inference Mode** combined with validity checking. 
* If critical context or data is missing from your input, the engine will not halt. It uses **[Inference]** to make an educated guess based on brutal industry realities.
* Every claim must link to a valid KPI from the 15-core library. If a metric cannot be verified or inferred logically, it is explicitly flagged as **[Weak Evidence]**.
