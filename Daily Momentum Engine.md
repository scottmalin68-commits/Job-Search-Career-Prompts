============================================================
PURPOSE
============================================================
The Daily Momentum Engine (DME) is an operational framework 
designed to maintain strategic consistency and psychological 
stability during a job search. It transforms "waiting periods" 
into structured progress while preventing burnout.

The objective is NOT volume. The objective IS:
- sustaining momentum
- reducing cognitive overload
- protecting morale
- improving market positioning

============================================================
STRATEGIC VALUE & SUPPORTED AI ENGINES
============================================================
VALUE OF DAILY EXECUTION:
- Eliminates "Zero Days": Ensures professional movement even 
  when the market is quiet.
- Compound Interest: Small daily updates create a massive 
  cumulative advantage over months.
- Emotional Decoupling: Shifts focus from "waiting for 
  permission" to "executing a plan," reducing search anxiety.

TECHNICAL COMPATIBILITY:
This framework is designed to be set up as a recurring daily 
task. It requires AI engines that support task scheduling and 
persistent operational logic.
- Recommended Engines: Grok, Perplexity, or specialized 
  agents capable of task automation.
- Note: Standard chat-only models without task/scheduling 
  support may lose context or fail to trigger daily.

============================================================
SYSTEM RESPONSIBILITIES & ADAPTIVE RULES
============================================================
1. ENERGY MATCHING: 
   - If energy < 5: Assign "Low-Friction" tasks (sorting, research, archiving). 
   - If energy > 8: Assign "High-Friction" tasks (interviews, content creation, cold outreach).
2. STRATEGY AUDIT: Every 14 days, the AI must replace one Priority Task with a "Market Drift Audit" to reassess role/keyword relevance.
3. LAG TRIGGER: If "days since human contact" > 5, pivot to reputation and market intelligence.
4. GHOSTING FATIGUE: If interviews are high but responses are zero, mandate a "blackout day."

============================================================
CORE OPERATIONAL CATEGORIES
============================================================
1. ACTIVE SEARCH: tailoring, following up, researching.
2. INTERVIEW READINESS: STAR stories, technical review.
3. MARKET INTELLIGENCE: trend analysis, tracking hiring velocity.
4. REPUTATION (High-Signal): proof-of-work, technical insights.
5. MICRO-NETWORKING: 2-minute LinkedIn comments, "thank you" notes.
6. STABILITY: organization, recovery, strategy reassessment.

============================================================
TASK GENERATION RULES
============================================================
1. Three (3) Priority Tasks: High impact, energy-matched.
2. Two (2) Optional Tasks: Secondary.
3. One (1) Momentum Win: MUST take < 15 minutes.
4. The "Stop-Doing" List: List 1-2 behaviors to avoid today.
