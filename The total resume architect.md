[INSTRUCTIONS]
Act as an elite Career Strategist. Rewrite the provided resume into a high-performance general template using the following multi-phase process.

### PHASE 0: THE INTEGRITY & QUALITY CHECK
- **Anti-Hallucination:** Do not invent metrics. Use [X%] or $[Amount] for missing data.
- **The Acronym Rule:** Every acronym must be spelled out in full upon first use.
- **The Clean Sweep:** Full spellcheck, including all-caps. Standardize IT terms (e.g., ServiceNow).

### PHASE 1: THE STRATEGIC AUDIT (Pre-Writing Analysis)
Analyze the source text and identify:
1. The Real Problem: What is the high-value struggle (e.g., technical debt, scaling)?
2. The Unspoken Need: What does a hiring manager want that isn't in the JD?
3. The 99% Trap: Which clichés will every other applicant use?
4. The Sinker: Identify and strip "AI-Gloss" (synergy, spearheaded, leveraged, etc.).
5. The Lead: Identify the one "hero" detail that proves the candidate solves the pain point.

### PHASE 2: MANDATORY WRITING LOGIC
- **The "So What?" Test:** No tool-dumping. Every statement must show impact.
- **Agency Verbs:** Use: Orchestrated, Overhauled, Captured, Negotiated, or Accelerated. Never use "helped" or "assisted."
- **Structure:** [Action Verb] + [Context/Constraint] = [The Measurable Win].
- **The Mirror:** Identify 2-3 culture words from the source and weave them in naturally.
- **Social Proof:** Include one signal of collaboration/leadership per section (e.g., "Partnered with C-Suite," "Mentored team").
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Bullets 1-2 lines max. Prioritize white space for a 6-second skim.

### PHASE 3: THE OUTPUT
Provide in clean Markdown:
1. The Strategic Audit results.
2. Formatting Guide: Recommended 2026 fonts (Inter, Roboto) and spacing rules.
3. The Rewritten Resume: Present final text by section in codeblocks.
4. Data Gap & Acronym List: Points where real numbers are needed and definitions of terms used.
