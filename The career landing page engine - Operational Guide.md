# Operational Guide: The Career Landing Page Engine (v3.6.1)

### 1. THE ARCHITECTURAL GOAL
The "Professional Spotlight" is not a digital resume; it is a **Technical Briefing**. Its purpose is to prove you understand the company’s **Market Friction** and to provide a "B-Side" narrative that adds depth to your existing application.

---

### 2. THE GOOGLE SITES DEPLOYMENT (LOW-FRICTION HOSTING)
While the engine generates raw HTML/Tailwind code, hosting it on Google Sites ensures it passes through corporate email filters safely.

* **Step A:** Go to [sites.google.com](https://sites.google.com) and create a "Blank" site.
* **Step B:** Use the "Embed" tool from the right-hand menu.
* **Step C:** Select **Embed Code** and paste the entire HTML block generated by the Engine.
* **Step D:** Publish the site using one of the **4 Suggested URL Names** provided in the Engine's output.

---

### 3. THE PSYCHOLOGY OF BRAND MIRRORING
The engine offers to detect and apply the target company's brand colors. This is a deliberate **Cognitive Mirroring** tactic.

* **Why Use It:** Seeing familiar brand hex codes triggers "In-Group" bias. It makes your profile feel like an internal resource rather than an external "ask."
* **The Accessibility Guard:** The v3.6.1 logic automatically lightens dark brand colors (WCAG AA Compliance) to ensure they pop against the dark-zinc theme without sacrificing readability.
* **The Recommendation:** Always say "Yes" to branded mode unless the company brand is intentionally chaotic or clashes with a professional "PlainTalk" aesthetic.

---

### 4. THE "TABLE SETTER" (v1.8.1) INTEGRATION
The Spotlight is most effective when delivered 24-48 hours before an interview via the **Table Setter** prompt.

* **The Workflow:** Once your Google Site is live, copy the URL.
* **The Snapshot Update:** Open your `[JOB SNAPSHOT]` file and add this tag at the very bottom:
    ```ini
    [Career Landing Page]
    URL=[https://sites.google.com/view/your-custom-link](https://sites.google.com/view/your-custom-link)
    ```
* **The Execution:** When you run the **Table Setter (v1.8.1)**, the AI will see this tag and automatically weave a "Strategic Resource" bridge into your outreach emails.

---

### 5. DATA INTEGRITY: "TRUTH TAGGING"
To maintain a "Double-Track" strategy (where the Spotlight doesn't just repeat the resume), the engine uses:
* **VERBATIM:** Explicit matches between your profile and the JD.
* **INFERRED:** Value-add conclusions that bridge "The Gap" between your past wins and their current **Market Friction**.

---

### 6. SUMMARY OF THE LOOP
1. **RECON:** Run OSINT on the Job Snapshot to find the "Friction" and Brand.
2. **GENERATE:** Use v3.6.1 to build the HTML code and update your Snapshot file.
3. **DEPLOY:** Host the code on Google Sites.
4. **OUTREACH:** Use v1.8.1 to "Set the Table" with the live link included.
